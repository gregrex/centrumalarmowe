# First 10 Dispatch Rounds

1. Chest pain in residential zone — nearest ambulance available.
2. Small kitchen fire — fire truck delayed by traffic.
3. Street fight near stadium — police first, ambulance second.
4. False alarm smoke detector — verify and close.
5. Minor road collision — mixed response, low severity.
6. Power outage causing elevator entrapment — fire support.
7. Panic call from shopping mall — police support with uncertainty.
8. Child fainting at school — ambulance with medium urgency.
9. Fuel leak near road hub — fire plus traffic risk.
10. Multi-call overload in heatwave — coordinator triage challenge.

## Design goal
These rounds are short, legible and ideal for:
- mobile quick sessions,
- bot fallback testing,
- HUD tuning,
- map readability testing.
