# Acceptance — City Map, Dispatch, Timeline

## Acceptance checklist
- city map payload exists and parses,
- at least 5 nodes exist,
- at least 4 connections exist,
- at least 3 live units exist,
- at least 3 dispatch actions exist,
- timeline demo exists,
- dispatch endpoint contract exists,
- timeline endpoint contract exists,
- Unity has controllers for city map, units, incident card and timeline,
- V6 smoke scripts pass,
- docs cover readability, graphics, gameplay and bot fallback.

## Playability acceptance
A tester should understand:
- where the incident is,
- which unit is available,
- what action to take,
- what changed after dispatch,
- why the round report looks the way it does.
