# HUD Widgets, Overlays and Cards Prompts

## Master style
premium 2d emergency operations interface, readable mobile game HUD, layered card design, sharp icon silhouettes, serious civic emergency tone, clean hierarchy, high contrast critical alerts, premium gradients, subtle texture, no childish style, no clutter

## Prompt groups
1. Runtime HUD panels
2. Recovery decision cards
3. Objective chips
4. City pressure widgets
5. Route overlays
6. Fail/retry/next gates
7. Reward reveal ribbons

## Negative prompts
blurry ui, childish mobile game, toy-like icons, sci-fi holograms, low contrast, messy overlays, oversized text, random logos, unreadable route lines
