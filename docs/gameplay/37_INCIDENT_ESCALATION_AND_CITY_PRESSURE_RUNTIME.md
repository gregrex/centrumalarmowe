# Incident Escalation And City Pressure Runtime

## Pressure inputs
- liczba aktywnych incydentów,
- blokady tras,
- zużycie jednostek,
- pogodowe utrudnienia,
- media tension,
- liczba objective at risk.

## Escalation chains
- medical -> traffic jam -> delayed fire -> media spike,
- stadium crowd -> police overload -> city pressure spike,
- blackout -> route failures -> cascading objectives.
