# Role-Focused Audio Priorities

## Goal
Keep audio meaningful even when the screen is crowded.

## Operator priority
- new call alert,
- caller escalation,
- critical medical urgency,
- line drop warning.

## Dispatcher priority
- dispatch confirm,
- no unit available,
- route blocked,
- unit arrived.

## Coordinator priority
- multi-incident escalation,
- overload threshold,
- chain event trigger,
- KPI drop warning.

## Crisis officer priority
- infrastructure failure,
- reserve deployment,
- citywide alert,
- severe cluster warning.

## Shared rules
- critical beats override soft ambience,
- avoid long harsh loops,
- one short cue per important state change,
- timeline insert sound should be subtle,
- report reveal sound can be calmer and more reflective.
